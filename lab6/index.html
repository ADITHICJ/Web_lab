JSS Science and Technology University

Mysuru, Karnataka

JSS Science and Technology University (JSS STU) is one of the leading technical universities in Karnataka.

The university offers Undergraduate, Postgraduate and Doctoral programs in Engineering, Science, Management and Computer Applications.

JSS STU provides excellent infrastructure, experienced faculty, modern laboratories and a vibrant campus environment for students.

Facilities

  • Modern Laboratories
  • Digital Library
  • Sports Complex
  • Hostel Facilities
  • Placement Support

Vision: To become a globally recognized institution for excellence in education, research and innovation.
